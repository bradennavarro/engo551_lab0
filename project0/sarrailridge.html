Sarrail Ridge

Description

Sarrail Ridge via Rawson Lake Trail is located in Kananskis. The hike up to Rawson lake is considered moderate but the Sarrail Ridge hike is considered more challenging. Round trip time is 5-6.5 hours. Please note that this trail can be closed regularly throughout the hiking season due to high bear activity.
