Smutwood Peak

Description

Smuttwod Peak Trailhead is located in Kananaskis, Alberta. The trail starts off with a long flat trek to get to the start of the hiking trail. Round trip time is 5-8 hours. This hike is rewarding in September when the larches turn yellow and is a great opportunity to get stunning photos.
